Platform Capabilities

Automated Fairness & Bias Audits

Run real-time audits using industry-standard fairness metrics across demographic subgroups and use-case scenarios.

Scope & Use-Case Validation

Certify your models for specific, declared functions to prevent misuse and monitor for functional drift over time.

Explainability & Transparency

Generate model cards and SHAP-based visualizations to comply with explainability requirements across jurisdictions.

Security & Copyright Safeguards

Identify vulnerabilities, injection risks, and possible copyright violations using adversarial and LCS-based testing.

Lifecycle Certification & Monitoring

Integrate certification into your CI/CD pipeline with ongoing recertification triggers for retraining and production drift.

Multi-Standard Compliance Engine

Map audit results to global compliance frameworks like the EU AI Act, NIST RMF, and ISO/IEC 42001.

How It Works

Step 1: Upload your model and sample data. Declare the intended use-case and target population.
Step 2: Our platform runs fairness, robustness, explainability, and security diagnostics in a secure sandbox.
Step 3: We generate a machine-readable certificate, compliance dashboard, and optional public badge.
Step 4: Set triggers for recertification or integrate with your Git-based CI/CD pipeline.
